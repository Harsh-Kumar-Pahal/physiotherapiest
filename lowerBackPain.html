Lower Back Pain Treatment

Effective solutions to relieve back pain and improve your quality of life

Lower Back Pain

Lower back pain is a common ailment that affects millions of people worldwide. It can range from a dull ache to a sharp, intense pain and is often caused by muscle strain, poor posture, or underlying conditions. Treatment involves a combination of physical therapy, exercise, and lifestyle modifications.

Sciatica Treatment

Sciatica is a painful condition resulting from compression or irritation of the sciatic nerve. Symptoms include sharp pain, tingling, and numbness that radiates from the lower back to the legs. Treatment options include physical therapy, stretching exercises, and, in severe cases, surgical intervention.

Musculoskeletal Disorder

Musculoskeletal disorders affect muscles, bones, and joints, often leading to pain, stiffness, and reduced mobility. Conditions such as arthritis, tendonitis, and osteoporosis are common musculoskeletal disorders. Treatment may include physical therapy, medications, and lifestyle adjustments to manage symptoms and improve function.

Post Joint Rehabilitation

After a joint surgery or injury, post-joint rehabilitation is crucial for restoring mobility and strength. This process involves a personalized program of exercises and therapies designed to help patients regain their range of motion, prevent stiffness, and promote a full recovery.
